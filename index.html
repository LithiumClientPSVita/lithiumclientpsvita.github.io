How to install Lithium:

1st step: To install Lithium, make sure that the version chosen will not conflict with your current Minecraft version (Region ID).

2nd step: Download the chosen folder

3rd step: Slide the "Lithium_Install_Folder" and "Lithium_reFiles" folders to your PSVita with Vitashell.

4th step: Go inside the "Lithium_Install_Folder" folder, press triangle, go to "More" and choose the option "Install Folder".

5th step: Open the "Lithium_reFiles" folder, slide the "reAddcont" folder and the "rePatch" folder in the root of ux0.

6th step: Launch Lithium and enjoy!
